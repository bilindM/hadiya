Welcome to Hadya Organization

Hadiay organization for women’s right is Nongovernmental organization established in Oct. 2022 with the objective to promote gender equality and advance the rights of women and girls. This includes advocating for laws and policies that protect women from discrimination and violence, as well as ensuring that women have access to education, healthcare, and economic opportunities.
